Our Story

Shake-up opened in 2016 in Holborn, London.

Our customers are very busy professionals or students, who want to eat healthy, yet they value their time! To keep them healthy and happy, which is our main aim, we decided to open a shake bar, where you can find alternatives to your daily meals, all squashed into one cup! :) We give your body all beneficial ingredients it needs to life your life to the fullest.

Since opening, we have gained 60% of our loyal customer base. Our lovely staff knows them by their names!

We regularly revise our menu based on customer feedback and seasonal produce.

Our Ingredients

Our ethos is to create revitalising, healthy, yet tasty and nutritious shakes all suitable for Vegans. All products are made using the highest quality ingredients and keeping in mind the biological effect on the body. We do not use artificial additives or preservatives. We also focus on wheat-free, gluten-free, non-refined sugars and good fats for example; Coconut oil or avocado.

We believe in creating pioneering shakes with undeniably great tastes and textures. Our staff is super knowleadgeable about all our ingredients - please feel free to have a chat with them and ask questions!

All our products are made fresh to order.
