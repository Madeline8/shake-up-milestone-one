Our Story

Shake-up opened in 2016 in Holborn, London.

Our customers are very busy professionals or students, who want to eat healthy, yet value their time! Our aim is to keep them healthy and happy, and so we decided to open a shake bar, where alternatives to daily meals can be found, all squashed into one cup! We give your body all the beneficial ingredients needed to live your life to the fullest.

60% of our client base are loyal and returning customers. Our lovely staff knows them by their names!

We regularly revise our menu based on customer feedback and seasonal produce.

Our Ingredients

Our ethos is to create revitalising, healthy, yet tasty and nutritious shakes.

All products are made using the highest quality ingredients and we keep in mind the biological effect on our bodies. We believe in creating pioneering shakes with undeniably great tastes and textures.

Our staff are super knowleadgeable about all our ingredients - please feel free to have a chat with them and ask questions!

All our products are made fresh to order.
